Hi, I'm Britt.

I hold a Ph.D. in Neuroscience and Neuroengineering from the University of California, Davis, and throughout my career as a researcher, engineer, and open-source software developer, I have worked to foster scientific and technological innovation. I have built my career leveraging my interdisciplinary expertise to accelerate the impact and effectiveness of my colleagues and collaborators.

Specifically, my focus is on advancing the quality of the tools, methods, and technical infrastructure utilized by scientific and engineering organizations, with the goal of ensuring their long-term performance, productivity, sustainability, and compliance.

Outside of work, I'm a huge fan of movies, video games, D&D, and sci-fi/fantasy novels, and I'm always on the lookout for the perfect set of speakers or headphones.

Most importantly, I'm an open book. So please, ask me anything.
