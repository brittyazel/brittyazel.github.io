About me

I'm a Ph.D. student at U.C. Davis, and I am well underway into my research focusing on hearing loss and auditory selective attention.

By day I spend my time working, researching, and running the day to day operations of the Miller Lab in the Center for Mind and Brain. In my off time I like to spend my time in and around open source software and technology, as well as amateur woodworking.
